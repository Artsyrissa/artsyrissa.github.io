My Mission

I believe self expression is important for everyone! My goal is to help people express themselves through art, even if they cannot draw themselves. I make high quality vinyl stickers, prints, and custom pieces of art so you can express yourself visually. Put my stickers on water bottles, laptops, notebooks, and whatever else you want to everyday life more fun! Hang my prints on your walls for inspiration or simply just to make you smile. If you commission me, I can provide a customized illustration of whatever you want to visualize, but cannot produce yourself. Let’s make life artsy!

Values

I believe in quality, sincerity, novelty, and whimsy. These principles are what I live by and I strive to create every product to those standards. I use supplies of the highest quality and all my products are made with care and love. I hope to bring novelty and whimsy into all of my designs and allow you to visually express what you could not before.

My Experience

As a little girl, I always dreamed of becoming an artist and making people smile through my art. In high school, I decided I wanted to work in the animation industry. After two years at a community college, I was accepted into San Jose State’s prestigious animation/illustration program. I studied there for about 3 years, until I was forced to take a break from school to do unexpected circumstances. Although I am still working on my degree, I have accumulated many important skills from my time at university, and I implement those skills in my work today. I plan to return to finish my degree, as soon as I have the means to do so. From there, I hope to attain a job in the animation industry as a character designer. But, for now, I am happy making stickers and custom pieces for you to enjoy!
